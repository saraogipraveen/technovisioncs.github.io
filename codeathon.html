Introduction

It’s here. Total competition, one winner. Code-A-Thon is one of the biggest events of Technovision and one of its kind, will not only test your programming skills but your passion towards programming.
Let’s work those sticky keys and rack those brains! This is the ultimate test of your concentration skills.
Three rounds in total, each round will test your difficulties to the core.
Once the timer starts, you're on your own.
Do you have what it takes to be a good programmer ?
Come and put your skills to the test now.
Registration Amount: 20/-

Rounds

Round 1: It would be simple MCQ test consisting of 20 questions related to language you have chosen.

Round 2: This round would be more difficult then previous one. In this round participants have to debug errors as well correct the logic in the given piece of code.

Round 3:This round would be little tricky. Here participant will be given question or output will be shown and they have to type the whole code. Participants with least or no error will be the winner.

Rules

  • You can choose either C++ or Java language only.
  • Time limit will be provided in each round accordingly.
  • Participants are not allowed to bring any media devices such as pen drives, CD, DVD etc.
  • Use of cell phones is not allowed.
  • *Prize money will be transferred to your respective bank account.*
